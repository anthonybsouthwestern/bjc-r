For most of this assignment, we will be working with a dataset containing data about penguins. This Table has 333 rows and 7 columns.

To download this dataset, you can click this link.

This dataset contains 7 columns:

  1. Species: The species of the penguin
  2. Island: The Antarctic island the penguin comes from
  3. Bill Length: The length of the penguin's bill in millimeters
  4. Bill Depth: The depth of the penguin's bill in millimeters
  5. Flipper Length: The length of the penguin's flippers in millimeters
  6. Body Mass: The penguin's total mass in grams
  7. Sex: The sex of the penguin

Feel free to look through the dataset if you like, you can put the querying blocks you already to know to use!
