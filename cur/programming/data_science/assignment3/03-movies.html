For this assignment, we will be working with a dataset containing movie data. This Table has 1659 rows and 9 columns.

To download this dataset, you can click this link.

This dataset contains 9 columns:

  1. Year: The year the movie was released
  2. Length: The duration of the movie in minutes
  3. Title: The title of the movie
  4. Genre: The genre of the movie
  5. Actor: The movie's leading actor
  6. Actress: The movie's leading actress
  7. Director: The name of the movie's director
  8. Popularity: An integer between 0 and 100
  9. Awards: yes if the movie has won awards, and no if not
